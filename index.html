Come with us on a journey to the bottom of the page, and you'll learn what Twingl is all about.

Capture

Researching? Create trails, not tabs.

It's a familiar story. You search Google, follow a few links, and before you know it, fifty tabs are open and you're hopelessly lost.

Twingl takes those tabs and creates a map. It shows you where you are, how you got there, and what to look at next.

Never lose your place in tabs again.

Incubate

Visualise your knowledge.
Uncover new patterns.

Twingl grabs the metadata from the things you've saved, like author or topic, and automatically organises this stuff for you into a large map. You can even create your own links between related bits of content.

Twingl shows you a map of how these ideas connect and resurfaces old, forgotten gems when they're useful—helping you learn more deeply from the things you've been reading.

Create

Organise your projects at the speed of thought.

Having trouble pulling all of your ideas together?

Twingl makes it speedy to collate all of the information around your projects. You can branch notes out, following your train of thought, and Twingl will automatically suggest content from your library that it thinks will be useful.

Spend less time organising your stuff, and more time putting it to use.

Publish

Publish your process.
Learn from your readers.

If you publish online, Twingl can embed the trail of thoughts and readings that informed your post.

Build rapport with your readers by showing them how you arrived at a point, and learn from them. Your readers can suggest new links and readings that you may not have known about.

Twingl bridges the gap between you and your readers, and turns every blog post into a conversation.
