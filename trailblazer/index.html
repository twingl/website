Making online learning visible.

Trailblazer is a web browser that turns students' research trails into a browsable map. This visualisation keeps students on track, and provides teachers with a window into their students' process.


Come with us on a journey to the bottom of the page, and you'll learn what Trailblazer is all about.

Researching? Create trails, not tabs.

It's a familiar story. You search Google, follow a few links, and before you know it, fifty tabs are open and you're hopelessly lost.

Trailblazer takes those tabs and creates a map. It shows you where you are, how you got there, and what to look at next.

Never lose your place in tabs again.
